Our Story

Founded in 2018, Serinity Paws Pet Salon started with a simple mission: to provide a calm, natural, and earthy environment for pets to relax while they get pampered. We believe that grooming shouldn't be stressful. That's why we use only organic, earth-friendly shampoos and gentle handling techniques.
